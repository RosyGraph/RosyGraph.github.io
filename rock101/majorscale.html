Major Scale

Music Concepts Covered:

  • The major scale
  • Root notes
  • Intervals

Learning Objectives:

  • To understand the major scale and its formula
  • To understand root notes
  • To understand interval

The Major Scale:

  • Start by having the group sing "Do, re, mi, fa, sol, la, ti, do"
  • Explain that these are the 7 notes of the major scale
  • Give the class the "formula" for the major scale - WWHWWWH and repeat
  • Play them
  • Have the class play the major scale on one string
  • Have the class play and count the major scale
  • If guitarists know the "standard" two-octave shape, have them play that
  • Play the scale in different keys

Root Notes:

  • The first note is the tonic
  • This note names the scale and identifies the key
  • Also called the one

Intervals:

  • Interval definition
  • Half step vs whole step
  • Intervals measured in quantity
  • Explain 2nd, 3rd, etc
  • Play root note and ask for specific intervals
