Rock History

Music Concepts Covered

  • History of Rock music
  • Genres of Rock music
  • Band of the Week

Learning Objectives

  • To learn the basic history of Rock music
  • To learn about the different genres of Rock music
  • To learn about bands that are the featured Band of the Week

History of Rock Music

  1. White it is widely debated, most historians consider Rocket 88 by Jackie Brenston to be the first Rock song. It featured Ike Turner on the piano
  2. Play examples of Rock songs from various decades to give an idea of the chronology a. 50's - Chuck Berry, Elvis, Jerry Lee Lewis, Wanda Jackson b. 60's - The Beatles, Jimi Hendrix, Rolling Stones c. 70's - Led Zeppelin, Heart, Deep Purple d. 80's - Blondie, Talking Heads, The Clash e. 90's - Nirvana, Weezer, Breeders f. 00's - White Stripes, Black Keys, St. Vincent

Genres of Rock Music

  1. Ask the class to name as many genres of Rock as they can and write them on the board
  2. Try to name every genre that we study at SOR
    • Rock, classic rock, metal, punk, soul, blues, pop, alt, grunge reggae, funk
    • Subgenres
  3. Under each genre have them name bands that fit the genre

Band of the Week

  1. Indentify a band as "Band of the Week". Use "core" bands that we feature in shows
  2. Give the band history
  3. Play examples
  4. Quiz the students on facts about the band
